# Myrva

AI-powered parametric income insurance for India’s gig workers.

Myrva is designed for delivery partners (Swiggy / Zomato) whose weekly income is highly sensitive to disruptions outside their control, such as severe weather, hazardous air quality, and government-declared restrictions.

Traditional insurance is not suitable for this problem because the loss is temporary income disruption, not physical asset damage. Myrva solves this with **trigger-based, automatic payout logic**.

## Why Myrva exists

- Delivery partners earn only when they are online, receiving orders, and able to move safely.
- Disruptions can block one or more of those conditions immediately.
- Essential expenses (fuel, rent, food) continue regardless of disruptions.
- Manual claims are slow for weekly-income workers and reduce trust.

## What Myrva provides

- **One plan:** Delivery Partner Income Shield
- **Enrollment window:** 2 months
- **Payment cycle:** weekly premium
- **Payout mechanism:** automatic if trigger conditions are met and fraud checks pass
